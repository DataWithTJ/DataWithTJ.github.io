Housing Data Cleaning In SQL

In this data cleaning project, I focused on refining a large housing dataset from Nashville to enhance its usability for advanced analytics. My approach involved standardizing address formats, correcting inconsistent date entries, and removing duplicates to ensure data integrity. I also normalized values in categorical fields like 'LandUse' and 'SalePrice' for consistency. To deal with missing values, I employed strategies like imputation and exclusion, depending on the context. This meticulous cleaning process was crucial in preparing the dataset for predictive modeling and trend analysis, aligning with my role as a data scientist.
